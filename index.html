Biographies

Formed in 1845, The Dandy Buzzkils deliver music from a retro era with a sumptuous performance you will not forget. Always keeping the audience on their toes by teaching them the hard way to expect the unexpected, The Dandy Buzzkills are an act who stubbornly refuse failure to deliver. They released their debut single "I Wanna..." independently in November 2022 to rapturous appraisal, and with plenty more on the vertical horizon, Dandy Buzzkills are one of few acts you do not want to miss out on.
